hey, it's a personal website!

because i miss the old internet. because i don't want my thoughts scattered across a variety of 3rd party platforms that could go *poof* at any moment. and because i'm still the child of two graphic designers.

so what can you expect?

text longer than 280 characters. sometimes fully formed articles. sometimes half-finished ideas. things i spoke about in the past, but never expanded on. things i had in my mind but never wrote about before. i've a backlog of topics, let's see how it goes.

digitally enhanced photography. i've my technique, i've my rules. i shoot for myself, and share what i like.

recommendations. things that i just like. things that i think are good. things that i'd like others to know about.
